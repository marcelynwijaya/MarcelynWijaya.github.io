ABOUT US

UPH Medan Dance Club is a student activity unit from UPH Medan Campus. The club showcases various dance styles, including modern, traditional, hip hop, and other genres. For the 2024/2025 season, we have chosen red and gold as our theme colors. Red represents passion and energy, which are at the heart of every performance, while gold symbolizes excellence and the joy of sharing our art. We are grateful for the opportunity to serve through our dances and look forward to creating more memories and performing in more events.
